# Tata Capital — Eligibility Criteria & Document Requirements

*Last updated: February 2026 | Version 4.1 | Classification: Customer-Facing*

---

## 1. Personal Loan

### 1.1 Eligibility — Salaried Applicants

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Age | 21 years (minimum) to 58 years (at loan maturity) |
| Nationality | Indian Resident / NRI (select locations) |
| Employment Type | Permanent employee of a listed company, MNC, government, PSU, or Tata Capital-approved employer |
| Minimum Work Experience | Total: 2 years; Current employer: 6 months |
| Minimum Net Monthly Income | ₹25,000 (metro/Tier-1 cities); ₹20,000 (Tier-2/3 cities) |
| CIBIL Score | 700+ preferred; applicants with 650–699 may be considered with additional conditions |
| Existing Obligations | Total EMI/income ratio (FOIR) should not exceed 50%–60% |

### 1.2 Eligibility — Self-Employed Applicants

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Age | 25 years (minimum) to 65 years (at loan maturity) |
| Business Vintage | Minimum 3 years of continuous business operations |
| Minimum Annual Income | ₹3,00,000 (as per latest ITR) |
| CIBIL Score | 700+ preferred |
| Business Profitability | Should have declared positive net profit in at least 2 of the last 3 financial years |
| Entity Type | Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Private Limited (select cases) |

### 1.3 Documents Required — Salaried

**Identity & Address Proof (any one from each category):**

| Purpose | Accepted Documents |
|---------|-------------------|
| Identity Proof | PAN Card (mandatory), Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence |
| Address Proof | Aadhaar Card, Passport, Utility bill (not older than 3 months), Bank statement with address, Rent agreement with utility bill |

**Income Proof:**
- Latest 3 months' salary slips showing all components (basic, HRA, deductions, net pay)
- Form 16 / Income Tax Return for the latest financial year
- Bank statement of salary account for the last 6 months (must show salary credits clearly)
- Offer letter or appointment letter (if employment vintage < 1 year at current employer)

**Employment Proof:**
- Employee ID card (photocopy)
- Employment confirmation letter / HR letter (if salary slips are unavailable)

**Other:**
- 2 recent passport-size colour photographs
- Existing loan account statements (if applicable, for FOIR computation)
- NACH / ECS mandate form (provided by Tata Capital)

### 1.4 Documents Required — Self-Employed

**Identity & Address Proof:** Same as salaried applicants.

**Income & Business Proof:**
- Last 2 years' Income Tax Returns (ITR) with computation of income, complete with acknowledgement
- Last 2 years' audited financial statements (Profit & Loss Account, Balance Sheet)
- If turnover exceeds the audit threshold: Tax Audit Report (Form 3CD with annexures)
- Bank statements of the primary business account for the last 12 months
- GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)
- Shop and Establishment Act licence, Udyam Registration, or equivalent business proof
- Partnership Deed / LLP Agreement / Certificate of Incorporation (as applicable)

**Other:**
- 2 recent passport-size colour photographs
- Office/business premises address proof
- Existing loan account statements (if applicable)
- NACH / ECS mandate form

## 2. Business Loan

### 2.1 Eligibility

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Entity Type | Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Private Limited Company |
| Business Vintage | Minimum 3 years of continuous operations |
| Minimum Annual Turnover | ₹10,00,000 (₹10 lakh) |
| Profitability | Positive net worth in the latest financial year; net profit in at least 2 of the last 3 years |
| CIBIL / Commercial Score | 650+ for Individual; CIBIL Rank 1–5 for Company/Firm |
| Banking Habit | No cheque bounces or irregular transactions in the last 12 months |
| Industry | Must not be in Tata Capital's Negative List of industries (e.g., speculative activities, real estate speculation, arms/ammunition, political organisations) |

### 2.2 Documents Required

**Entity & Promoter KYC:**
- PAN Card of the business entity AND all proprietors / partners / directors
- Aadhaar Card of all proprietors / partners / directors
- Passport-size photographs of all proprietors / partners / directors

**Business Registration:**
- Certificate of Incorporation (for companies), Partnership Deed, LLP Agreement, Sole Proprietorship Declaration
- GST Registration Certificate
- Udyam / MSME Registration (if applicable)
- Trade licence / Shop and Establishment licence
- Import-Export Code (IEC) — if the business is involved in foreign trade

**Financial Documents:**
- Last 3 years' Income Tax Returns (business entity and individual promoters)
- Last 3 years' audited financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, schedules, notes)
- Tax Audit Report (Form 3CD) — if applicable
- Bank statements of the primary business account for the last 12 months
- Debtor-creditor ageing schedule (if loan amount > ₹25 lakh)
- Projected financials for the next 1–2 years (if loan amount > ₹50 lakh)

**Collateral Documents** (for secured business loans):
- Property documents (title deed, approved building plan, tax receipts, encumbrance certificate)
- Valuation report from empanelled valuer
- NOC from society / builder (if applicable)

## 3. Home Loan

### 3.1 Eligibility — Salaried

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Age | 23 years (minimum) to 62 years (at loan maturity, maximum maturity age 65) |
| Employment Type | Permanent employee; contract employees considered case-by-case |
| Minimum Work Experience | Total: 3 years; Current employer: 1 year |
| Minimum Net Monthly Income | ₹30,000 (metro); ₹25,000 (non-metro) |
| CIBIL Score | 700+ preferred |
| Property | Must be in a Tata Capital-approved location; clear title; construction as per approved plan |
| LTV Ratio | Up to 80% for loans up to ₹30 lakh; up to 75% for loans ₹30–75 lakh; up to 70% for loans > ₹75 lakh |

### 3.2 Eligibility — Self-Employed

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Age | 25 years (minimum) to 70 years (at loan maturity) |
| Business Vintage | Minimum 5 years of continuous operations |
| Minimum Annual Income | ₹5,00,000 |
| CIBIL Score | 700+ preferred |
| Property | Same property requirements as salaried applicants |

### 3.3 Documents Required

**Applicant/Co-Applicant KYC:**
- PAN Card (mandatory for all applicants and co-applicants)
- Aadhaar Card / Passport / Voter ID
- Passport-size photographs

**Income Documents:**
- *Salaried*: Latest 6 months' salary slips, Form 16, bank statements (6 months)
- *Self-Employed*: Last 3 years' ITR, audited financials, bank statements (12 months)

**Property Documents:**
- Registered Sale Agreement / Agreement to Sell
- Title Deed (chain of title documents demonstrating clear ownership for at least 30 years or since land acquisition by the current title holder chain)
- Approved Building Plan (sanctioned by the relevant municipal authority — BMC, BBMP, GHMC, etc.)
- Commencement Certificate and Occupancy Certificate (or expected OC date for under-construction properties)
- Encumbrance Certificate (EC) for at least the last 15 years
- Property Tax Receipts (latest year)
- NOC from Housing Society / Builder / Land Authority (as applicable)
- Allotment Letter (for under-construction properties from the builder)
- Possession Letter (for ready properties)
- Builder-Buyer Agreement (if applicable)
- RERA Registration Number of the project (mandatory for under-construction properties, as per Real Estate Regulation and Development Act, 2016)

**Other:**
- Existing loan account statements (for all running loans, to compute FOIR)
- NACH mandate form
- Processing fee cheque / online payment receipt

## 4. Loan Against Property (LAP)

### 4.1 Eligibility

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Applicant Type | Salaried or self-employed individual; companies and firms also eligible |
| Age | 25 years (minimum) to 65 years (salaried at maturity) / 70 years (self-employed at maturity) |
| Property Type | Residential or commercial property that is free from any existing mortgage/lien |
| Property Location | Must be in a Tata Capital-approved city/zone |
| LTV Ratio | Residential: Up to 65%; Commercial: Up to 55% (based on market value as assessed by empanelled valuer) |
| Minimum Property Value | ₹25,00,000 |
| Income Requirement | Adequate disposable income to service proposed EMI (FOIR < 60%) |

### 4.2 Documents Required

All documents listed under Home Loan (applicant KYC, income, property) **plus**:

- Registered ownership documents demonstrating the applicant's clear title to the property
- Latest Valuation Report from a Tata Capital-empanelled valuer
- Existing loan account statements on the property (if any prior mortgage exists — this must be settled before or at the time of LAP disbursement)
- NOC from existing lender (if the LAP involves a balance transfer)
- Property insurance policy (may be required for commercial properties)

## 5. Consumer Durable Loan

### 5.1 Eligibility

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Age | 21 years (minimum) to 60 years |
| KYC | Valid Aadhaar + PAN |
| Income | Regular source of income (salaried or self-employed) |
| Product | Must be purchased at an authorised Tata Capital partner retail store or e-commerce platform |
| Down Payment | 0%–20% depending on product and scheme |

### 5.2 Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card and PAN Card
- 1 passport-size photograph
- Cancelled cheque or bank passbook front page (for NACH setup)
- No additional income proof required for loan amounts up to ₹1,00,000 at select partner stores (subject to instant credit assessment)

## 6. Education Loan

### 6.1 Eligibility

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Applicant | Student who is an Indian national |
| Co-Applicant | Parent, guardian, or spouse (mandatory) |
| Course | Full-time graduate, post-graduate, or professional course at a recognised university/institution in India or abroad |
| Admission | Confirmed admission / offer letter required |
| CIBIL Score (Co-Applicant) | 650+ preferred |
| Collateral | Unsecured: Up to ₹7.5 lakh; Secured (collateral required): Above ₹7.5 lakh |

### 6.2 Documents Required

**Student:**
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card
- Mark sheets (10th, 12th, Graduation — as applicable)
- Admission offer letter / confirmation letter from the institution
- Course fee structure from the institution
- Passport and Visa documents (for overseas education)
- GRE/GMAT/IELTS/TOEFL score card (for overseas education)

**Co-Applicant:**
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card
- Income documents (salary slips / ITR / financials — same as Personal Loan requirements)
- Bank statements for 6–12 months
- Property documents (if pledging collateral for secured education loan)

## 7. Important Notes on Documentation

1. **All documents must be self-attested** by the applicant / co-applicant.
2. **Originals must be produced for verification** at the time of login. Tata Capital will retain photocopies / scanned copies.
3. **Bank statements must be official** — either original bank-stamped or downloaded from net banking with the bank's digital signature/watermark.
4. **Address proof must be current** — utility bills accepted only if not older than 3 months from the date of application.
5. **For NRI applicants**: Passport, Visa, work permit, overseas bank statements, and employment contract are additionally required.
6. **Additional documents** may be requested during credit assessment based on the applicant's profile or product-specific requirements. Tata Capital reserves the right to request any additional information deemed necessary for the underwriting process.
7. **Incomplete documentation** will delay processing. Applicants are encouraged to provide all listed documents at the time of application to ensure fastest processing.
